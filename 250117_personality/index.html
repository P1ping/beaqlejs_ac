Thank you for participating in this listening test. The purpose of this listening test is to conduct surveys regarding personalities in conversation. We provide two experiments regarding conversation personalities. The first one is to relate the personalities to conversation traits (e.g. how “extraversion” is related to “frequent laughters”). The second experiment is to ask you to determine the personalities of two speakers by listening to 90-seconds dialog between two speakers.
Instructions:
  • Please make sure you are using high-quality headphones and are in a quiet environment.
  • If you need to leave halfway, just keep the page open. Also, you can submit the results after each sub-test.
  • There is a slider at the bottom of each test page that can be used to change the playback range.
  • If you encounter problems with the audio playback, try switching browsers.
  • After finishing each test, please submit the result with your name and your email address.
  • It contains 2 listening tests in total.
  • Please carefully read the definitions of the personalities we will cover (available at the end of this page.)
  • In Second experiment, each participant is assigned to different test link. Please make sure you enter the correct test link.
Five Personalities we cover in this survey. Please take screenshot or put the definitions aside in case you forget it.
  • "Openness": intellectual, imaginative, independent-minded; opposite term is "Closeness".
  • "Conscientiousness": orderly, responsible, dependable; opposite term is "Lack of direction".
  • "Extraversion": talkative, assertive, energetic; opposite term is "Introversion".
  • "Agreeableness": good-natured, cooperative, trustful; opposite term is "Antagonism".
  • "Neuroticism": emotional instability, irritability, anxiety, self-doubt, depression; opposite term is "Emotion stability".
